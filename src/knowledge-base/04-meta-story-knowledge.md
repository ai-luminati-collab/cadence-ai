# META STORY: Platform-Native Content Knowledge
## Platform: Instagram/Facebook | Format: Story | Last Updated: April 2026

---

## DRAFT FIELDS FOR AI GENERATION

1. **Story Copy**: Primary text/message on the story. Short and scannable in 2 seconds. Conversational tone. Written the way the brand would text a friend.

2. **Media Type**: Video or static image. Video stories get 1.4x more forward taps (people actively engage). Static stories get more replies and are easier to produce.

3. **Interactive Feature**: Which Instagram feature to use. Options: Poll, Quiz, Countdown, Question Box, Emoji Slider, Link Sticker, Mention Sticker, Add Yours Sticker.

4. **Feature Configuration**: Configuration details for the chosen feature. Poll: two options. Quiz: question + answer options with correct answer marked. Countdown: event name + end date. Question prompt: the specific question to ask.

5. **Visual Directive**: Look and feel guidance. Lo-fi vs polished. Sticker and text placement. Font selection from Instagram's options. Color palette alignment with brand. Background treatment (solid color, image, video).

6. **Reply CTA**: How to encourage DM replies. A specific prompt that invites a response. DM conversations are a top algorithm signal for relationship strength. Example: "Reply with your favorite one" or "DM us 'GUIDE' and we'll send it."

## RESEARCH-BACKED RULES

### Stories vs Reels vs Feed Posts
- Stories serve EXISTING audience (followers who already engaged with the brand)
- Reels expand to NEW audience (discovery-focused, shown to non-followers)
- Stories are for: timely, ephemeral, interactive content and daily brand touchpoints
- Best uses: behind-the-scenes moments, polls and Q&A, countdown to launches, flash sales, daily engagement, audience research, driving DM conversations

### Interactive Features Are Mandatory
- Stories with interactive stickers get 2-3x more engagement than stories without them
- Polls and quizzes drive the highest interaction rates
- Question boxes generate DM conversations, which are a top algorithm signal for relationship strength
- Countdown stickers create anticipation for launches, events, and drops
- Link stickers drive traffic for conversion-oriented stories (product pages, blog posts, sign-up pages)
- Emoji sliders are low-effort engagement that still signals interaction to the algorithm

### Frequency and Cadence
- 3-7 stories per day is the optimal range for brand accounts
- Post in sequences: a story arc of 3-5 stories that tell a mini-narrative is more effective than isolated single stories
- Do not front-load all stories at once; space them throughout the day to maintain presence in the stories tray
- Consistency matters: posting stories daily keeps the brand in the top row of the follower's stories tray

### Story Arc Structure (for multi-story sequences)
- Story 1: Hook/setup (grab attention, establish context)
- Story 2-3: Develop (deliver content, show process, build interest)
- Story 4: Interact (poll, quiz, question box)
- Story 5: Payoff/CTA (reveal, link, next step)
