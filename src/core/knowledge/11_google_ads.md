## The Power Pack (2026 Framework)

Google's recommended campaign architecture, replacing the Power Pair:
1. Demand Gen: Creates awareness and interest across YouTube, Discover, Gmail, Display, and now Google Maps. 26% increase in conversions per dollar via 60+ AI improvements.
2. AI Max for Search: Suite of features applied to Search campaigns (broad match technology, keywordless targeting, text customization, URL expansion). Not a new campaign type.
3. Performance Max: Orchestrates full-funnel performance across all Google properties simultaneously.

## Performance Max (2026)

PMax uses Google AI across five dimensions: targeting, bidding, creative, placements, and budget allocation.

Key 2026 updates:
- Experiments: Test PMax against Shopping/Search/Display, test URL expansion, test incremental lift
- First-party audience exclusions: Exclude customer lists to focus on acquisition
- Full audience reporting: Detailed performance by age and gender
- Network segmentation in placement reporting for brand safety
- Campaign total budgets: Fixed budget for defined flight periods (open beta for Search, PMax, Shopping)
- Minimum 30+ conversions/month per campaign for reliable optimization
- Video importance growing: 25-40% better performance with comprehensive video vs. image-only
- Shopping overlap identification across account
- Performance Planner no longer supports Display/Video campaigns (March 2026)

PMax optimization areas:
- Signal design: Require click attribution for conversions to eliminate view-through noise
- Campaign structure: Only segment if each campaign gets 30+ conversions monthly
- Asset quality: Google's AI assigns performance ratings. Low-performing assets drag down campaigns.
- Test different tones (urgent vs. conversational vs. authoritative) across assets

## Demand Gen (2026)

No longer just "Discovery with a new name." Major changes:
- Now includes Google Maps (promoted pins) in inventory
- Actively competes for conversion credit (moved toward mid-to-low funnel)
- Brand search action: New metric for brand searches after ad exposure
- YouTube engagement goals for subscriptions/channel interactions
- New customer acquisition goals
- Target CPC bidding option available
- Advertisers adopting 3+ of 4 best practices see 40%+ more conversions
- Best practices: Optimized targeting + Lookalikes, tCPA/tROAS bidding, "Excellent" Ad Strength, sitewide tagging + Data Manager

Video Action Campaigns upgrading to Demand Gen by April 2026.

## AI Max for Search Campaigns

Features you can apply to existing Search campaigns:
- Broad match technology with keywordless targeting (DSA-style)
- Text Customization for AI-generated ad copy
- URL Expansion for automated landing page selection
- Brand guidelines to constrain AI-generated text

## Google Ads Best Practices (2026)

- Use click-only attribution for conversions to eliminate view-through noise
- Consolidate campaigns (fewer, better-funded > many small ones)
- Creative quality equals campaign management importance. Refresh static every 4-6 weeks, video every 8-12 weeks.
- Budget allocation: 70% proven performers, 20% optimization, 10% testing new approaches
- Don't fight automation; design better signals
- AI Max text guidelines should be implemented before scaling AI-generated creative
- Campaign total budgets for seasonal/promotional flights
- VRC Non-Skip for YouTube/CTV brand campaigns
