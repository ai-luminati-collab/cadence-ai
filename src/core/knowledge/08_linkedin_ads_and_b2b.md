## Platform Positioning

LinkedIn has 1 billion+ members globally, including 67 million decision-makers. 80% of B2B leads from social media come from LinkedIn. LinkedIn-sourced leads convert at 3x the rate of leads from other platforms. 82% of B2B marketers report greatest success on LinkedIn.

---

## Algorithm (2026)

- Authenticity Score: Detects engagement pods, automated comments, inauthentic activity. Penalties are dramatic.
- Content lifespan: Posts have 24-hour half-life (vs. X's 18 minutes or Facebook's 5 hours).
- Video growth: Short-form video (7-15 seconds) increasingly effective.
- Multi-image posts drive highest likes; polls drive highest impressions.
- Personal accounts outperform company pages for engagement.

---

## LinkedIn Ads Best Practices (2026)

Targeting: Precision targeting by job title, company size, industry, seniority, skills, specific companies. Avoid hyper-targeting (restricts delivery, raises costs).

Campaign structure by funnel:
- TOFU: Thought leadership, trend reports, benchmarks, short videos. Ungated value.
- MOFU: Lead gen forms tied to webinars, checklists, calculators. Document Ads perform well.
- BOFU: Direct demo bookings, free trial CTAs, case studies.

Cost benchmarks: CPCs 3-5x higher than Google Ads. CPLs $60-$150 for standard B2B SaaS. Enterprise targeting often exceeds $200. Budget minimum: $1,500-3,000/month for sufficient optimization data.

Creative rules:
- Short, direct headlines ("2026 SaaS Benchmarks" beats "Our Comprehensive Guide to SaaS Trends")
- Real people build trust faster than stock photos
- Lead with value to the reader, not product features
- Test CTAs by funnel stage

---

## Key 2026 Tactics

- Thought Leader Ads: Boost employee content. Much higher engagement than company page ads. Humor works from personal accounts.
- Qualified Lead Optimization: LinkedIn's CAPI equivalent. Sync CRM data to help algorithm find higher-quality leads.
- Short-form video (7-15 seconds) in feed
- Document Ads for TOFU engagement
- CTV capability (testing phase)
- Ads duplication feature for operational efficiency
- First Impression Ads (newer video placement)

---

## LinkedIn Organic

- 3-5 high-quality posts/week outperforms daily low-effort content
- Consistency in topic builds authority
- Unique point of view drives engagement (contrarian perspectives backed by data)
- Develop recognizable signature style in posts
- Content pillars: educational insights, industry commentary, personal stories, data-driven analysis
- Creator mode activation for follower growth

---

## ABM on LinkedIn

Build campaigns around account lists, not demographic profiles. Align messaging to where accounts are in their research. Target entire buying groups (6-10 decision-makers), not single personas. Combine with intent data for timing.
