## The Andromeda Algorithm

Meta's ad delivery system, called Andromeda, completed its global rollout in October 2025. It runs 100x faster and processes 10,000x more ad variants than the previous system, using NVIDIA GH200 chips. In practice, this means Meta tests more creative combinations, finds audiences faster, and improves personalization significantly.

Key implication: Creative is now the primary targeting signal. Under Andromeda, your ad creative tells the algorithm who to show it to. Diverse creative (different hooks, formats, angles) effectively serves as audience segmentation.

## Advantage+ Campaign System (2026)

As of 2026, Advantage+ is the default setup for Sales, Leads, and App Promotion objectives. When creating a new campaign, the following are pre-selected: Advantage+ Audience (broad targeting; your inputs are treated as suggestions, not restrictions), Advantage+ Placements (all placements enabled), Advantage+ Creative enhancements (auto-brightness, aspect ratio, text overlays), and Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO).

March 2026 updates:

- Audience suggestions: Meta shows performance impact estimates when you add audience suggestions
- Conversion threshold reduced to 25 conversions/week for Advantage+ Shopping (down from 50)
- First-party audience exclusions: Exclude specific customer lists
- 47 new advertising policy rules, with AI transparency disclosure required for AI-generated creative assets
- Graph API v25.0 deprecates Advantage Shopping Campaigns (ASC) and Advantage App Campaign (AAC) APIs, requiring migration to unified Advantage+ campaign API

## Campaign Structure Best Practices (2026)

1. Consolidate campaigns: Fewer campaigns with higher budgets per campaign. Fragmented structures actively harm performance under Andromeda.
2. Creative diversity over creative volume: Andromeda values diversity of concept, not just volume of variations. Running 20 variations of the same image with different text overlays is penalized (high "Creative Similarity" score raises CPMs).
3. Full-funnel in one campaign: The algorithm identifies retargeting vs. prospecting users automatically. Complex manual funnels are often unnecessary.
4. Minimum 5 ad creatives at launch: Mix of product-focused video, lifestyle imagery, and text-overlay static ads.
5. Let campaigns run 7+ days before evaluating.
6. Scale budget by 10-15% every 48 hours, not in big jumps.
7. Vertical-first creative: 90% of Meta's ad inventory is now vertical. Produce 9:16 as primary, 4:5 as secondary. Never rely on Meta's auto-crop.
8. Learning phase requires 50 optimization events/week.

## Creative Strategy Under Andromeda

- UGC dominates: Authentic, raw, creator-style content consistently outperforms polished studio ads.
- Creative fatigue is the biggest enemy: Rising CPMs are the primary signal. Refresh every 2-4 weeks.
- Cross-format repurposing: Winning video? Extract the hook for a static. Winning static? Turn headline into a video hook. Goal is concept diversity across formats.
- AI-generated content requires disclosure: Meta's review system catches undisclosed AI content. Violations cause ad disapproval or account flags.
- Creative Similarity score: If high (meaning you lack diversity), Andromeda raises CPMs because it views content as repetitive.
- 15-50+ active creatives needed for the algorithm to optimize properly.
- Brands should run major sitewide promotions at least 4x per year for scaling profitability.

## Attribution in 2026

Meta uses modeled attribution post-iOS 14/ATT. True ROAS is often 20-40% higher than Meta-reported ROAS. The engaged-view threshold was reduced from 10 to 5 seconds, changing reported conversion numbers.

The "Conversions (Platform Comparable)" column enables cross-platform performance comparison.
