# Case Study Library Structure and Recurring Reports
> Topic: Case study schema, B2B and D2C seed brands, must-ingest reports, official platform feeds
> Layer: Evidence and Intelligence Feeds
> Freshness: Q1 2026
> Confidence: High (LinkedIn, Meta, Shopify, Google case study sources)

---

## Case Study Schema

Every case study must follow this structure:
- Brand: Company name
- Category: Industry/vertical
- Geography: Market(s)
- Problem: Challenge faced
- Objective: What they set out to achieve
- Channel Mix: Platforms/channels used
- Creative Type: Formats/styles deployed
- Audience Strategy: Targeting approach
- Measurement Method: How success was measured (platform-reported, lift study, MMM, etc.)
- Outcome: Quantified results
- Why It Worked: Strategic insight
- What Is Transferable: What other brands can apply
- What Is NOT Transferable: Context-specific factors not to blindly copy (this field matters a lot)
